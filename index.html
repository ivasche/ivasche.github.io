Alexey Ivashchenko

Bio

I am an Assistant Professor of Finance at VU Amsterdam, School of Business and Economics. I earned my PhD in Finance from the University of Lausanne and the Swiss Finance Insitute. My research interests are in empirical asset pricing and market microstructure.
